Cake

This is one of my favourite recipe


List of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate flavour
  • Cake fruit (optional)

Steps/Method of preparation

  1. Put your desired measurement of butter in a clean dry bowl
  2. Pour your desired measurement of sugar and mix well till it gets fluffy,soft and has a smooth surface
  3. Add your egg together with your flavour and cake fruit
  4. Add your flour and mix well
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degrees celsius for 15 minutes
Serve and Enjoy!

Doughnut

This is one of my favourite recipe


List of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps/Method of preparation

  1. Put your desired measurement of flour, baking powder, and salt in a clean dry bowl
  2. Pour your desired measurement of eggs, sugar, and vanilla in a large mixing bowl. Beat until thick. In a small bowl combine milk and melted butter
  3. Add the flour mixture and milk mixture alternately to the egg mixture, beating after each addition just until combined. Cover and chill the dough for 2 to 4 hours.
  4. Fry the doughnut
Serve and Enjoy!
